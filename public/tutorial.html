Cook Out: Tutorial

Learn how to earn points, complete quests, cook delicious dishes and earn friendship!
1. Register & Login
  • Create an account
  • Login
2. Completing Quests
  • The quest page shows quests that you can complete.
  • Completing quests earns you points and ingredients to buy recipes.
  • You can also create quests of your own.
  • View quests you created and completed by clicking view challenge records
3. Buying Ingredients & Recipes
  • Recipes and ingredients are needed to cook dishes which can be bought in the shop.
  • Change the input field and enter the quantity you would like to buy and click buy
4. Inventory
  • The items you bought and got from completing challenges can be found here.
  • Dishes cooked can also be found here
5. Cooking
  • Using your recipes and ingredients, you can cook recipes turning them into dishes.
6. Feeding
  • The dishes cooked can be fed to Snorlax to satisfy his hunger.
  • Be careful not to feed Snorlax something he doesn't like
  • Feeding Snorlax gains you friendship points.
  • Make sure to feed Snorlax frequently or he will be angry.
